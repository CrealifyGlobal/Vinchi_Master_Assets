
# Designed for Strategic, Technical, and AI-Enabled Roles

---
## Phase 1 – Job Role Framing

### Purpose: Understand the role, context, and language.
- **Input:** Full job description (or PDF).
- **GPT will generate:**
    - Keyword analysis (Primary / Secondary / Skill-Based)
    - Role theme alignment (e.g., knowledge systems, AI, proposal automation)
    - Strategic framing: What the company wants + what the role represents
**Output:**  
→ A keyword map, job insights table, and role framing summary

---
## Phase 2 – Experience Alignment**
### Purpose: Map your experience to job requirements.
- **Input:** Upload resume(s), Obsidian notes, or detailed role/project history.
- **GPT will generate:**
    - Experience alignment table: High Match, Moderate Match, Potential Gaps
    - Recommendations for keyword enhancement
    - Tailored language suggestions for alignment (e.g., "proposal automation," "sales enablement")
**Output:**  
→ A structured experience alignment sheet that informs resume and cover letter tailoring

---
## Phase 3 – Core Messaging Blocks
### Purpose: Build reusable, high-impact personal narrative components.
**Blocks Developed:**
1. **Why This Position?** – Role fit, motivation, and strategic excitement
2. **Tell Me About Yourself** – Professional snapshot, career arc, domain authority
3. **Discussion Topics & Questions** – Strategic questions to ask in interviews
**Output:**  
→ Modular text blocks to plug into your resume, cover letter, interview scripts, or Notion profiles

---
## Phase 4 – Resume Creation (Using Approved Format)

### Purpose: Generate a full resume aligned to your approved structure.

**Structure:**
- **Header** (Name, Email, Phone, LinkedIn)
- **Professional Summary** (role-aligned, keyword-rich)
- **Key Skills Section** (grouped by theme and role relevance)
- **Professional Experience**
    - Company | Role Title | Dates
    - Subheader: “Relevant Experience”
    - 3–5 bullets directly tied to the role
- **Education**
- **Tools, Technologies & Methodologies**

**Deliverable:**  
→ Complete resume, formatted and ATS-friendly, with modular bullets for easy reuse.

---
## Phase 5 – Cover Letter Creation (Using Approved Format)**

### Purpose: Create a concise, targeted, and ATS-optimized cover letter.

**Structure:**
- **Header** (Personal and Contact Info)
- **Opening Paragraph** (intent and alignment)
- **Experience Bullets** (4–5 achievements relevant to the job)
- **Narrative Paragraph** (“Why this position?” block from Phase 3)
- **Closing & Signature**

**Deliverable:**  
→ Cover letter ready to copy/paste or export to Word.
