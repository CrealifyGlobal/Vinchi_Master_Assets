# Application Analysis, Cover Letter & Resume Generator (v2)
**Designed for Strategic, Technical, and AI-Enabled Roles**

This manual guides GPT through a multi-phase process to analyze job descriptions, align your experience, and generate tailored resumes and cover letters — all optimized for ATS systems and strategic messaging.

---

## ⚙️ Overview of the Process
GPT will complete the following phases:

| Phase | Title                                | Purpose                                                                 |
|-------|--------------------------------------|-------------------------------------------------------------------------|
| 0     | Baseline Job Description Capture     | Create a perfect Markdown copy of the job description you provide.     |
| 1     | Job Role Framing                     | Understand the role, company, and strategic context.                   |
| 2     | Experience Alignment                 | Map your experience to the role requirements.                          |
| 3     | Core Messaging Blocks                | Build reusable narrative components for tailoring.                     |
| 4     | Resume Creation                      | Generate a structured, ATS-ready resume.                               |
| 5     | Cover Letter Creation                | Generate a concise, role-specific cover letter.                        |

Each phase builds on the previous and outputs content you can use across job applications, networking, and interview prep.

---

## Phase 0 – Baseline Job Description Capture  
**🌟 Purpose:** Create a clean, reusable, and perfectly exact Markdown version of the job description you provide — for storing in folders or use across Obsidian workflows.

### 🔁 GPT Instructions:
1. **Introduce the Process Briefly**  
   GPT should recap:
   > "Thanks for sharing this manual. I’ll walk through each of the six phases starting with Phase 0 (Job Description Capture), then move through framing, experience alignment, messaging, resume, and cover letter creation. Let’s begin with Phase 0."

2. **Request Input:**  
   GPT asks:
   > “Please paste the full job description exactly as written (copied from the job posting or PDF).”

3. **Markdown Output Formatting:**
   - **Retain the text 100% exactly** — no changes, no editing, no rewording.
   - Format the content as a Markdown file with a level 1 header at the top: `# Job Description – [Job Title or Placeholder]`
   - Ensure line breaks and section dividers from the original are preserved.

4. **Output Example:**
   ```
   # Job Description – Growth & Proposal Knowledge Lead

   Arcadis is seeking a strategic professional to join our...
   [rest of job description, exactly as pasted]
   ```

5. **Deliverable:**  
   → A ready-to-copy Markdown version of the original job description for archival, versioning, or use in other systems.

## Phase 1 – Job Role Framing  
**🌟 Purpose:** Understand the strategic context, language, and implied priorities of the role.

### 🔁 GPT Instructions:
**Input:** Full job description (already captured in Phase 0)

**GPT Will Generate:**
- **Keyword Analysis**  
  - Primary Keywords (frequently used, high-signal words)
  - Secondary Keywords (contextual/related terms)
  - Skill-Based Keywords (tools, platforms, functions)

- **Role Theme Alignment**  
  - Identify core themes (e.g., AI systems, proposal automation, cross-functional enablement)

- **Strategic Framing**  
  - What does this role *represent* to the company?
  - What problems is this role solving?
  - What strategic initiatives does it likely support?

**Deliverables:**
→ A keyword map, a job insights table, and a role framing summary  
→ These are used in Phase 2 and Phase 3

---

## Phase 2 – Experience Alignment  
**🌟 Purpose:** Map your experience against the job description to identify strong fits and gaps.

### 🔁 GPT Instructions:
**Input:** Resume(s), Obsidian notes, LinkedIn export, or a detailed project history.

**GPT Will Generate:**
- **Experience Alignment Table:**  
  Categorize elements of your experience as:
  - High Match
  - Moderate Match
  - Potential Gaps

- **Recommendations for Keyword Enhancement:**  
  Suggest ways to add missing but relevant keywords into your experience sections.

- **Tailored Language Suggestions:**  
  Propose phrasing that matches the role's language (e.g., “proposal automation,” “sales enablement”).

**Deliverables:**
→ A structured alignment sheet  
→ Specific bullet ideas for use in resume or cover letter tailoring

---

## Phase 3 – Core Messaging Blocks  
**🌟 Purpose:** Build strong, reusable narrative components tailored to this role and your career arc.

### 🔁 GPT Instructions:
**Input:** Experience Alignment output + Strategic framing

**GPT Will Generate:**
- **“Why This Position?” Paragraph:**  
  Your motivation, strategic interest, and excitement for the role

- **“Tell Me About Yourself” Paragraph:**  
  Your professional story arc, role alignment, and domain strengths

- **“Discussion Topics & Questions” Paragraph:**  
  Strategic questions you could raise in interviews to show depth and engagement

**Deliverables:**
→ Modular narrative blocks for use in:
  - Cover letters
  - Interview prep
  - LinkedIn summaries
  - Notion profiles

---

## Phase 4 – Resume Creation  
**🌟 Purpose:** Create a complete, structured, and ATS-ready resume aligned to the job description and messaging blocks.

### 🔁 GPT Instructions:
**Input:** Approved structure + aligned bullet points from Phase 2 and Phase 3

**Structure Template:**

1. **Header**  
   Name | Email | Phone | LinkedIn

2. **Professional Summary**  
   - Role-aligned, keyword-rich snapshot

3. **Key Skills Section**  
   - Grouped by theme and relevance

4. **Professional Experience**  
   Company | Role Title | Dates  
   _Subheader:_ “Relevant Experience”  
   - 3–5 bullets directly tied to the job

5. **Education**

6. **Tools, Technologies & Methodologies**

**Deliverable:**  
→ A finalized, ATS-optimized resume with modular content for easy reuse

---

## Phase 5 – Cover Letter Creation  
**🌟 Purpose:** Write a clear, concise, and ATS-friendly cover letter that aligns with the role’s priorities.

### 🔁 GPT Instructions:
**Input:** Messaging blocks from Phase 3

**Structure Template:**

1. **Header:**  
   Name | Contact Information

2. **Opening Paragraph:**  
   - Intent and alignment with the role

3. **Experience Bullets:**  
   - 4–5 high-relevance achievements that match the role

4. **Narrative Paragraph:**  
   - Use the “Why This Position?” block

5. **Closing & Signature**

**Deliverable:**  
→ A complete, ready-to-use cover letter  
→ Modular content that can also be used for outreach or networking
